How do we work?

eBookPiles is a service that allows you to download ebooks in your prefered format, by subscribing. The eBooks can either be read directly on the website or application, or directly downloaded on your device, at your preference. The subscription allows you to get 2 or more free ebooks per months and to get special promotions on certain books!

Package details

Newbie package

Newbie is the basic package that we offer, placed at 9,99€ per month. This subscription grants you 2 free eBooks per month, and special reductions that change every month. We recommend this package for people that just got into reading or just want to try and see how they like eBookPiles.

Midkid package

Midkid is the intermediate package that we offer, placed at 14,99€ per month. This subscription grants you 3 free eBooks per month, and special reductions that change every month. We recommend this package for people who really like to read but don't have enough time on their hands to read everything they want to.

Veteran package

Veteran is the advanced package that we offer, placed at 19,99€ per month. This subscription grants you 5 free eBooks per month, and special reductions that change every month. We recommend this package for avid readers that devour books at the speed of light.

Bookworm package

Bookworm is the most crazy package that we offer, placed at 24,99€ per month. This subscription grants you 7 free eBooks per month, and special reductions that change every month. We recommend this package for readers who prefer reading to sleeping at night.
